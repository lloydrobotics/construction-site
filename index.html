About Us

For over 20 years, The Eagle Brothers have been transforming homes in the North London and Barnet & Enfield community with quality craftsmanship and dedicated service. We are a family-run business, driven to provide the highest level of detail and professionalism in the home-building space.

Our mission is to give you the exact home you want, with a focus on quality, care, and attention to detail. We partner closely with local gyms and fight clubs to support our community, offering apprenticeships and training for young people.

Meet our team:

  • Parryk Piasecki - Leader, Project Manager, Carpenter. Specializes in Carpentry and Interior Decorating.
  • Robert Elliot - Specialty in Plumbing and Heating. Over 15 years of experience in domestic plumbing systems.
  • Joshua Lloyd - Specialty in Marketing and IT. Manages marketing and communication within the organization.

Our Services

At Eagle Brothers Construction, we offer a wide range of services to meet your home-building needs:

  • Bathroom & Kitchen Installation - Transform your spaces with precision and to your exact specifications.
  • Plumbing & Heating - Expert installation, repair, and maintenance of domestic plumbing systems.
  • Carpentry & Interior Decorating - Quality craftsmanship for your home's interior.
  • All Stages of Construction - From tear down to tiling, we handle every step with care.

We are passionate about building your quality home with the highest level of detail and professionalism.
